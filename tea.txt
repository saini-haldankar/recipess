Classic Milk Tea 

Ingredients

1 cup (240 ml) water
½ cup (120 ml) milk
1 teaspoon black tea leaves (or 1 tea bag)
1–2 teaspoons sugar, to taste

Instructions

Bring the water to a boil in a small saucepan.
Add the tea leaves (or tea bag) and simmer for 2–3 minutes.
Pour in the milk and bring the mixture back to a gentle boil.
Add sugar and stir until dissolved.
Strain into a cup (if using loose tea leaves) and serve hot.

Optional flavor additions

2–3 lightly crushed Cardamom pods for a fragrant taste
A small piece of fresh Ginger for a spicy kick
A small stick of Cinnamon for warmth
A pinch of Masala chai spice blend for a richer flavor
