Fresh Orange Juice

Ingredients

4 fresh oranges
1–2 teaspoons sugar or honey (optional)
Ice cubes (optional)
A pinch of salt (optional)

Instructions

Wash the oranges and cut them in half.
Squeeze the juice using a citrus juicer or by hand.
Strain the juice if you prefer it without pulp.
Add sugar, honey, or a pinch of salt if desired, and stir well.
Pour over ice and serve immediately.

Optional variations

Add a little Lemon juice for extra tang.
Blend with Mango for a tropical twist.
Mix in fresh Mint leaves for a refreshing flavor.
Add a small piece of Ginger for a spicy kick.
