. The Easiest Instant Coffee (Cafe-Style)You don't need a machine for a rich cup of coffee. 
The secret to making instant coffee taste smooth and less bitter is mixing it with a tiny bit of cold water first.
Ingredients:
1 to 2 teaspoons of instant coffee powder
1 to 2 teaspoons of sugar (adjust to your taste)
1 tablespoon of cold water
¾ cup of hot water or hot milk
