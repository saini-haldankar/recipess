Coffee Recipe

Ingredients:
- 1 cup milk (or water)
- 1 teaspoon instant coffee powder
- 1-2 teaspoons sugar (adjust to taste)

Instructions:
1. Heat the milk (or water) until hot but not boiling.
2. Add the instant coffee powder and sugar to a cup.
3. Pour the hot milk (or water) into the cup.
4. Stir well until the coffee and sugar dissolve completely.
5. Serve hot and enjoy!
