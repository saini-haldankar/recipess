Coffee Recipe

Ingredients:
- 1 cup milk (or water)
- 1 cup almond milk
- 1 cup toned milk
- 1 teaspoon instant coffee powder
- 1-2 teaspoons sugar (adjust to taste)
- Chocolate (optional)

Instructions:
1. Heat the milk (or water) until hot but not boiling.
2. Add toned milk and almond milk.
3. Add the instant coffee powder and sugar to a cup.
4. Pour the hot milk mixture into the cup.
5. Add chocolate to the coffee.
6. Stir well until the coffee and sugar dissolve completely.
7. Serve hot and enjoy!
